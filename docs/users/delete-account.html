Account & Data Deletion

You may request the permanent deletion of your ML Toolkit account and all data associated with your ML User ID. This process is irreversible.

User-Controlled Privacy Permanent Deletion

What Will Be Deleted

  • Points and rewards balance
  • Daily task history and logs
  • Orders and redemption records
  • Points change logs
  • User cache and analytics identifiers

Once processed, deletion cannot be undone.

Exceptions

Some non-personal aggregated statistics may be retained, such as general usage counts. These do not contain identifiable information.

How to Request Deletion

  • In-app: Settings -> Delete Account
  • Email: devcloper.url@gmail.com (include ML User ID)
