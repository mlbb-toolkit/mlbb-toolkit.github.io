We are dedicated to keeping your data minimal and secure. ML Toolkit does not collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or email address.

The collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. No data transmission over the internet or storage system is 100% secure, but we do our best to safeguard it.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to request access to or deletion of any information we may have collected from your device. You may contact us directly to make such a request.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to us at support@mltoolkit.app.
