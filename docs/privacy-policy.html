Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 29, 2025

We value your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it while you use ML Toolkit.

Transparent practices User-first controls

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect only the data necessary for providing and improving the ML Toolkit experience. The information we collect includes:

  • Non-personal information: Device model, OS version, app usage statistics, and crash logs via trusted services like Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.
  • Account-related data: If you participate in our reward program, we may store your user ID, reward progress, Shard Points, and redemption history through our backend provider (Supabase).
  • Email information: Required only when redeeming rewards. Your email is used solely to send digital receipts or order confirmations — never for marketing.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To maintain and improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To fix bugs, optimize performance, and enhance stability.
  • To securely process reward redemptions and confirm orders.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party platforms for analytics, backend services, and user engagement. These include but are not limited to:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics – for app performance monitoring.
  • Supabase – for secure management of reward data, task logs, and user sessions.

Each service adheres to its own privacy and data protection practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for further details.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures such as encrypted communications (HTTPS) and protected databases. While no system can guarantee 100% security, we continuously update our measures to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or loss of your information.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Users can request the deletion of their stored data (including Shard Points and reward logs) by contacting us directly.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 16 and above. Users below this age must have parental consent to participate in any reward-related activities. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 16 without consent.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Limited data may be shared with service providers (e.g., Firebase or Supabase) solely to maintain core app functions. These partners are obligated to handle data securely and only as instructed.

8. International Data Transfer

Some of our service providers may store or process data outside your country. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data remains adequately protected.

9. Your Rights

  • Access and request a copy of your data.
  • Request correction or deletion of stored information.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

10. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect app updates or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available within the app or on our official website, with the effective date updated accordingly.
