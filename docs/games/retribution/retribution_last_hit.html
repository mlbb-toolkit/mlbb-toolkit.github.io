Retribution Master

Master the art of last-hitting jungle objectives using Retribution. This tool replicates real MLBB timing mechanics to help junglers secure objectives with consistent precision and reaction time.

How it works

  • A mini Lord appears with 10,000 HP.
  • Your Retribution damage is based on a random hero level (Lv. 4–15).
  • The Lord takes damage over time from simulated “team damage.”
  • Once the Lord becomes killable, the HP bar glows red.
  • Your goal is to time your Retribution perfectly to secure the kill.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Team damage is applied every 400 ms.
  • Kill-window opens when Lord HP ≤ your Retribution damage.
  • Retribution cast too early instantly ends the round.
  • Particles play on a perfect Retribution kill.
  • Manual restart ensures consistent practice flow.

Retribution Result States

  1. Perfect: You cast Retribution exactly when the Lord becomes killable. A burst effect plays as confirmation.
  2. Too Early: You cast Retribution before the kill-window. The round ends immediately.
  3. Missed: The Lord dies from team damage before you cast Retribution.

Pro Tips

  • Watch the HP bar — it turns red in kill-range.
  • Learn the approximate thresholds for each Retribution level.
  • Don’t spam! Expect the kill-window and react naturally.
  • Try practicing at different hero levels for variety.
  • Focus on consistent timing rather than rushing.
