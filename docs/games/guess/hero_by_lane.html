Game Guide

Guess the Hero by Lane

Assign each displayed hero portrait to the correct lane before the countdown ends.

5 lane options 5-second rounds Practice friendly
How to play Round rules Scoring Pro tips Strategy

How it works

  • A hero portrait appears at the top of each round.
  • Choose from EXP Lane, Gold Lane, Mid Lane, Jungle, or Roam.
  • Select the lane that best fits the hero's playstyle and role.

Round rules

  • Each question has a 5-second time limit.
  • Questions advance immediately after your tap.
  • Incorrect answers display the correct lane for quick learning.

Scoring breakdown

  • Correct answers: Remaining time converts to points.
  • Fast accuracy: Higher streaks boost leaderboard scores.

Pro tips

  • Gold Lane: Marksmen and late-game carries.
  • Mid Lane: Mages needing fast rotation.
  • EXP Lane: Bruisers and sustain duelists.
  • Jungle: Assassin core heroes.
  • Roam: Tanks and heavy supports.

Leaderboard strategy

  1. Memorize lane identities to cut decision time.
  2. Stay updated on metas since lane priorities shift per patch.
  3. Tap calmly; rushed mistakes cost streak accuracy.
