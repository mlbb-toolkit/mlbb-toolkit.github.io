Guess the Hero by Lane

Assign each hero portrait to its best lane before the countdown ends.

5 lane options 5-second rounds Practice-friendly
How to play Round rules Scoring Pro tips Strategy

How it works

  • A hero portrait appears at the top of each round.
  • Choose from EXP Lane, Gold Lane, Mid Lane, Jungle, or Roam.
  • Select the lane that best fits the hero's kit and role.

Round rules

  • Every question has a 5-second limit.
  • Rounds advance instantly after your selection.
  • Incorrect answers reveal the preferred lane so you can learn quickly.

Scoring breakdown

  • Correct answers: Remaining seconds become score points.
  • Lane awareness: Faster decisions lead to higher leaderboards positions.

Pro tips

  • Remember marksmen thrive in Gold, mages in Mid, tanks/supports in Roam.
  • EXP lane favors durable duelists; assassins often start in Jungle.
  • Use the timer glow as a signal to commit when unsure.

Leaderboard strategy

  1. Drill lane basics to remove hesitation on common picks.
  2. Review patch metas so lane preferences stay current.
  3. Keep calm — confident taps maximize time-based scoring.
