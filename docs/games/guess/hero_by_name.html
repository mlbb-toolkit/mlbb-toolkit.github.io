Game Guide

Guess the Hero by Name

Test your MLBB knowledge by identifying heroes using only names. Each round challenges your speed, memory, and recognition skills.

5-second rounds 4 options per round Leaderboard scoring
How to play Round rules Scoring Pro tips Strategy

How it works

  • Each round displays one MLBB hero.
  • Choose the correct hero name from four options.
  • Select your answer before the countdown ends.

Round rules

  • 5 seconds per round.
  • New question appears automatically after your pick.
  • Wrong answers show the correct hero before moving on.

Scoring breakdown

  • Correct answers: Remaining time is added to your score.
  • Streaks: Consecutive correct rounds increase your leaderboard potential.

Pro tips

  • Watch the timer color change as the final warning.
  • Learn distinct hero traits for faster recognition.
  • Use each revealed answer to improve your memory.

Leaderboard strategy

  1. Balance speed and accuracy for higher bonus points.
  2. Practice regularly to improve hero recognition.
  3. Warm up with casual games before going competitive.
