Guess the Hero by Role

Identify each hero's primary role from the displayed portrait before time runs out.

6 role options 5-second rounds Instant feedback
How to play Round rules Scoring Pro tips Strategy

How it works

  • A single hero portrait appears each round.
  • Choose from Tank, Fighter, Marksman, Mage, Assassin, or Support.
  • Select the matching role before the timer ends.

Round rules

  • Every question runs on a 5-second countdown.
  • Answers lock in immediately, moving you to the next hero.
  • Misses reveal the correct role so you can memorize it.

Scoring breakdown

  • Correct answers: Remaining seconds are added to your score.
  • Role mastery: Quick recognition of class archetypes yields higher streaks.

Pro tips

  • Look for armor, weapons, and silhouettes to separate Tanks, Fighters, and Assassins.
  • Mages and Supports often feature magical effects or staffs — spot these quickly.
  • Use the timer glow as a cue to commit when you're between two roles.

Leaderboard strategy

  1. Group heroes by visual archetype during practice rounds.
  2. Drill role-specific quizzes to reduce hesitation on edge cases.
  3. Keep a steady rhythm: quick but confident taps maximize time bonuses.
