Guess the Hero by Role

Identify each hero's primary role by examining the displayed portrait before time runs out.

6 role options 5-second rounds Instant feedback
How to play Round rules Scoring Pro tips Strategy

How it works

  • A single hero portrait appears each round.
  • Choose from Tank, Fighter, Marksman, Mage, Assassin, or Support.
  • Select the correct role before the countdown ends.

Round rules

  • Each question runs on a 5-second timer.
  • Rounds advance immediately after selecting an answer.
  • Incorrect answers reveal the correct role to improve your recognition.

Scoring breakdown

  • Correct answers: Remaining time becomes score points.
  • Role mastery: Fast recognition boosts your leaderboard streaks.

Pro tips

  • Look for armor density, weapon type, and body size to spot Tanks and Fighters.
  • Mages and Supports often feature robes, orbs, staffs, or magical effects.
  • Marksmen have ranged weapons; Assassins use dual blades or agile gear.
  • Use the timer's color shift as your cue to commit quickly.

Leaderboard strategy

  1. Group heroes by visual archetype during warmups.
  2. Practice role quizzes to eliminate hesitation on close-call heroes.
  3. Maintain a steady rhythm to maximize time bonuses every round.
